About

With over 10 years of experience as a front-end developer, I have worked with some of Copenhagen's most innovative agencies and clients, delivering code that meets the highest standards of quality, performance, and accessibility. I've contributed code both to small-scale campaigns, as well as complex 30+ component corporate pages for industry leaders such as Microsoft, Vestas, Orsted, Danske Bank, Novo Nordisk, Optimizely, Telia, and others.

Some of the skills and competencies that set me apart from other developers are my keen interest in refactoring legacy SCSS, my extensive knowledge of accessibility rules and tips, and my recent exploration of sustainable web development and design. I am also passionate about bridging the gap between design and development and fostering a collaborative and inclusive culture within and across teams. My goal is to create web solutions that are functional, user-friendly, ethical, and environmentally conscious.
